Research Highlights

Equation Discovery: Developing a hybrid loss minimization framework using Local Polynomial Regression to recover hidden differential laws from noisy, time-indexed observations.

Phylogenetic Inference: Engineering high-dimensional MCMC algorithms to reconstruct evolutionary lineages and assess model sensitivity within complex biological spaces.

Predictive Stability: Implementing a novel mixture estimator for LOO-CV that ensures finite variance and robust out-of-sample accuracy for Bayesian predictive density.
